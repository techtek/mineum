About

The Mineum project started in October 2020 it's goal is to bring the Solana blockchain to more users and to get the new users it will send out rewards to the most committed users.

The Mineum application is made available for multiple devices and systems, and to make it easy to join it requires only a Google account and a free Solana wallet. The development of Mineum comes forward out of the Solana hackthon and the teaming up of Techtek, you can know him from Hive and Steem projects, and Aiell0 IT Cloud Architecture Consulting, and a Solana investor and Solana discord user since X.

To be able to develop the Mineum project, we will use 25% of the Hackathon rewards to fill up 52 weeks of rewards. 25% of the Hackathon rewards will be used for new developments and investments such as a own Solana Validator node. The rewards made with the validtor will be partialy be used to fill the weekly pool and this will help to grow the project into its full potential and run for a longer time and have it's own funding.
